Frequently Asked Questions

Can Airimg access my existing Google Drive files?

No. Airimg requests only the `drive.file` OAuth scope, which permits access exclusively to files uploaded by Airimg itself. It cannot read, list, or touch any of your existing personal files or folders.

Are there any subscription fees or hidden costs?

Airimg is 100% free and open-source. Images store in your personal Google Drive account, utilizing your standard 15GB+ free Google quota.

How does EXIF & GPS metadata stripping work?

Your browser loads the image onto an HTML5 Canvas and re-renders raw pixel data into a WebP file before network upload. This process completely strips EXIF data, camera info, and GPS location tags.

How resilient are the generated hotlinks?

Our Cloudflare Worker edge layer includes a circuit breaker and 4-tier fallback system (Path A → Path B → Path C → Path D) to keep your hotlinks active even during Google API degradation.
