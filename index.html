FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know

Can Airimg access my existing Google Drive files?

No. Airimg strictly requests the `drive.file` OAuth scope. This scope only permits access to files uploaded by Airimg itself. It cannot read, list, or touch your existing documents or photos.

Are there any subscription fees or storage limits?

Airimg is 100% free with no monthly fees. Images are saved to your personal Google Drive, giving you full control over your 15GB+ storage quota.

How does EXIF & GPS metadata stripping work?

Before sending any byte over the network, your browser loads the image onto an HTML5 Canvas and re-encodes pixel values into a WebP blob. This process discards all camera model, location, and metadata tags.

What happens if Google hotlink endpoints break?

Airimg's Cloudflare Worker features a built-in circuit breaker and 4-tier self-healing fallback system (Path A → Path B → Path C → Path D) so your image links stay online even if Google's public hotlink endpoint degrades.
